The Importance of Pilot Advocacy and Union Representation

Pilots play a crucial role in global aviation safety and efficiency. However, without strong union representation, pilots often face unfair labor practices, reduced job security, and a lack of negotiating power. The International Pilots Representation Group (IPRG) is dedicated to promoting fair treatment, safety, and professionalism for pilots worldwide.

Why Unions Matter in Aviation

From negotiating fair wages to ensuring safe working conditions, unions have been pivotal in enhancing the aviation industry's standards. Union representation has led to improvements such as:

  • Better rest policies, reducing pilot fatigue and enhancing safety.
  • Fair and transparent pay structures across different airlines.
  • Protection against unjust terminations and contract changes.
  • Advocacy for mental health and well-being programs.

Our Mission at IPRG

The IPRG strives to unify pilots globally, providing a platform for a collective voice that influences policy-making, enhances workplace safety, and promotes fair labor practices. By fostering a culture of safety and respect, IPRG aims to uplift pilots' standards and contribute positively to the aviation industry's future.

How You Can Support

Whether you are a pilot, aviation professional, or an advocate for labor rights, your support can make a difference. Join us in advocating for a safer, fairer aviation industry where pilots' voices are heard, and their contributions are valued.
