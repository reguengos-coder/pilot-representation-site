Examples of Union Wins for Pilots

Improved Working Conditions

  • Delta Air Lines (ALPA): Higher pay scales and improved scheduling, reducing pilot fatigue and promoting safety.
  • Ryanair (RPG): Secured better contracts, fairer rostering, and transparent career progression paths.
  • Lufthansa (VC): Enhanced pensions and job security during industry crises.
  • Emirates (EU): Advocated for fair working hours and pilot rest policies.
  • Qatar Airways (QPA): Achieved improvements in accommodation standards and pay transparency.

Examples of Union Wins in Cargo Airlines

  • FedEx Express (ALPA): Secured significant pay increases and implemented fatigue management programs.
  • UPS Airlines (IPA): Achieved a comprehensive contract with enhanced retirement benefits and improved work-life balance policies.
  • Atlas Air (IBT): Negotiated fair pay scales and better health and safety protocols.
  • Cargolux (CLPA): Introduced a profit-sharing scheme and improved transparency in career progression.
  • Challenge Airlines BE (Local Belgian Unions): Advocated for fair contracts and safe working conditions.
  • Challenge Airlines IL (Israeli Unions): Implemented fair compensation practices and supported pilot safety initiatives.
  • Challenge Airlines MT: Lack of union representation has led to challenges in negotiating pilot rights and benefits.
